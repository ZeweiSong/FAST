Analysing Fungal Amplicon Sequencing Data

Zewei Song

Intorduction

I wrote this pipeline protocol to make my life easier. Like everyone who just start to analyse his/her first next generation amplicon dataset, I spent many hours sitting in front of my laptop, trying to figure out which file I should use to feed the program. Besides banging my head against my office wall, I ended up writing a package of Python scripts that is used in this pipeline, can indeed make my life easier.

FAST (Fungal Amplicon Sequencing Toolbox) provides a framework for analysing the huge sequence data. I wrote it in order that a new user can go step by step from the raw data to an OTU table. The work flow in this pipeline represent a natural flow of processing the next gen data. I try to let a new user to have an idea of what they are doing at each step, and also get some information on the outcome of their command. I hope a fresh user will not just get a final product through this pipeline, but can also get a better idea of how the analysing works.

FAST also give some flexibility to experienced users.
